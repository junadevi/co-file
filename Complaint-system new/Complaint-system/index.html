About e-complain

e-complain is a web-based platform designed to facilitate the reporting of various community issues such as water supply problems, electricity outages, gas concerns, and road conditions. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly interface that allows citizens to easily report issues and track their resolution.

We believe in empowering communities by providing a transparent and efficient way to address local problems. By using e-complain, you can help us improve the quality of life in your area.
